  V 1.9.0  06/06/2016

What's New 

Revision operation rules applied to the previous 30 day follow-up 

  • Revision operation < 90 days and Unplanned, previous follow-up will update the Sentinel Event to "Unplanned return to theatre", Follow-up value to "Auto Complete", Follow-up Date to "Revision operation date" and Reasons (Complications)
  • Revision operation < 90 days and Planned, previous follow-up will update Follow-up value to "Auto Complete" and Follow-up Date to ""Revision operation date""
  • Revision operation > 90 days, nothing will be updated

Surgical reversal operation rules applied to primary operation annual follow-up's 

  • All annual follow-up's will be cancelled unless they are submitted

Revision operation after surgical reversal operation rules applied 

  • Revision operation date determines the annual follow-up's are due for later or leave as abandoned unless they are submitted
  • Routine schedule task will update the follow-up's due or not due every night

Legacy patient Reasons (complications) updating is fixed 

Security fix has been updated (Missing security headers)
